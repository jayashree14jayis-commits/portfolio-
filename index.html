03 — Design Process

A process I trust for every project.

A repeatable rhythm I follow from a blank page to a finished screen — on every project, including Cast Connect and Vela Senja.

Discover

Problem framing, quick research, market scan.

Define

Pain points, goals, what success looks like.

Ideate

Sketches, mood boards, quick concepts.

Design

Wireframes, UI screens, a consistent system.

Prototype

Clickable flows to test before final build.

Test

Feedback loops, quick honest iteration.

Deliver

Polished files, notes, a case study write-up.
